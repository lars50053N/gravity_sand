Gravity Sand is a Falling Sand Game, where you can control the direction and strength of gravity inside the simulation.

This can be done either by adjusting it manually through sliders or by automatically gathering the acceleration values provided by the device's motion sensor.

Gravity Controls

You can use these controls to adjust the gravity inside the simulation.

In Manual Mode, gravity is controlled by these sliders.

In Automatic Mode, gravity is determined by your device's sensors.

Brush Size

You can adjust the amount of sand that will be added to the simulation when clicking on the canvas.

Sand Type

You can choose, how the sand should behave when lying on the ground.

Dynamic Sand will start to disperse, when piled up on the ground. The rate of dispersion is dependent on the gravity's strength.

Static Sand will not disperse and only move in the gravity's direction.
